About Us

Began with a Cart.

This was something Gokul Kuppuram would often miss – his love for spicy and tangy Indian chats. After completing his graduation in Mechanical Engineering in Bengaluru, Gokul was all set to get into routine engineering jobs. However, not finding a suitable job profile in the market forced him to change tracks. Being upset by the turn of events along with the desire to do something of his own, Gokul decided to look at something he loved, his family served over years - chats.

A chats lover by blood took family entrepreneurship, made up his mind to switch fields from engineering to sell Pani Puri's in 2018. After a year of struggle, he setup and renovated his father's pani puri cart to chats truck under the brand name, Naidus Chats in Basaweshwarnagar, Bengaluru. The chats truck offers variety of chats like Masala Puri, Bhel Puri, Paani Puri, and others.

People love Indian food and people in bengaluru crave for good chats. However, one of the biggest challenges Gokul faced was the inflated costs of raw materials, and setting up of the chats truck. He found it more difficult as he had bootstrapped his business by taking money from friends and family.

From the typical pani puri cart to chats truck, the brand is now seeing a good revenue and has received good response from customers.

“When I started my business, people questioned my choice of domain and what was a highly-educated person doing in a traditional field like this. But I was strong headed in my dream of making this business a success and a professionally run venture, and I am still working towards my goal,” says Gokul Kuppuram.

Idea of chats truck

The thought of chats truck was born on a street in Bengaluru. With over thirty years of serving the traditional chats, we opened our chats truck.
